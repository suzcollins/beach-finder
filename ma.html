Beaches in Massachusetts

Coast Guard Beach

Eastham MA

Open all year round, its a great clean beach for swimming during the summer. Parking area is closed to all but park staff and visitors who are disabled, and access is provided by shuttle bus from the Little Creek parking area. However the parking lot is open the remainder of the year, but parking is limited. There are bathrooms and a wheelchair accesible ramp to the beach. If lucky, one may be able to spot seals during low tide.

Long Beach

Gloucester, MA

Long Beach is located directly next to Good Harbor Beach. Surfing and boogie boarding is allowed all day at Long Beach and life guards are on site. You can rent paddleboards, $30 for an hour or $50 for half a day. Parking is avaiable but price varies. This beach is the perfect beach for families who want to relax on land or water. There are no resturants directly on the beach so either bring your own lunch or snacks or take a quick walk to Surfside Subs.

Wingaersheek Beach

Gloucester, MA

Wingaersheek is probably the best beach for families in Massachusetts. The water is very calm with hardly any waves. There are beautiful rocks and boulders in the water that are fun to climb on and searching for hermit crabs or sand dollars. There are lifeguards at this beach. There is snacks and ice cream located on the beach but it would be smart to pack lunch. There are also showers to rinse off in after a long day at the beach. The sand is smooth and is a bit more peaceful then other beaches in Gloucester.

Crane Beach

Ipswich, MA

Located in Crane Estate of Ipswich, it's filled with miles of soft white sand. From sand castle building to hiking through miles of dunes, theres millions of activities. Including available tours of the luxurious seaside mansion named Castle Hill. The beach is open year long and dogs are allowed from October 1 through March 30. Lifeguards will be present during the summer season. As well as bike racks, bathrooms, outside showers, picnic tables and the Crane Beach Store whichg sells souviners and snacks. transportation for mobility-impaired is also offered.
